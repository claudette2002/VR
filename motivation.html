Motivations behind the project

Lets face it before doing any reasearch I had no idea what VR was ,but going through my research it is quite interesting. The world around us is moving at such a vast pace and I was like "WOW", why not educate others too? My motivation comes from my own lack of knoweledge and i wanted to eduate the people of SA, because they deserve to have some sort of understanding of what the future could turn out to be.

So lets learn more about this new innovation called virtual really.Dont be one that is lost and confused but be filled with knoweledge and understanding about the world around you.Lets get educated about the future and try our utmost best to utilise our knowledge to our benefits.

We should keep up with the latest technology because without us even knowing it is indeed taking over and we should use all the knowledge we can get to equipt our selfs so that we are ready for it all.I have developed this site to educate people with basic knowledge and to show them the new perspectives and ideas.Do not forget to note the benefits this could bring us.So lets get educated together.
